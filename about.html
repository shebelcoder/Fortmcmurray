About Arrow Cab

Fort McMurray's premier taxi service, connecting communities across Alberta for over a decade

Our Story

Founded in Fort McMurray, Arrow Cab has grown from a small local taxi service into Alberta's most trusted long-distance transportation provider. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide safe, reliable, and affordable transportation to the hardworking people of Fort McMurray.

Over the years, we've expanded our services to cover the entire province of Alberta, from the oil sands camps in the north to Calgary and Lethbridge in the south. We understand the unique transportation needs of our communities - whether it's getting workers to remote camps, families to airports, or connecting loved ones across long distances.

Today, Arrow Cab operates 24/7 with a modern fleet and professional drivers dedicated to your comfort and safety. We're not just a taxi service - we're your transportation partner, committed to getting you where you need to go, when you need to be there.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Safety First

Your safety is our top priority. All our vehicles undergo regular maintenance, and our drivers are professionally trained and fully licensed.

Customer Care

We treat every passenger like family. From booking to drop-off, we ensure your experience is comfortable, friendly, and stress-free.

Reliability

Count on us to be there when you need us. We pride ourselves on punctuality, consistency, and dependable service you can trust.

Innovation

We embrace technology to make booking easier, routes smarter, and your experience better with modern solutions and online convenience.

Community

We're proud to be part of the Fort McMurray community, supporting local events, businesses, and the people who make our region strong.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in every ride, maintaining high standards for our vehicles, drivers, and customer service.
