Fleet Images
============

Add high-quality images of your taxi fleet:

- sedan.jpg - Standard sedan vehicle
- suv.jpg - Premium SUV
- van.jpg - Large van for groups

Recommended specs:
- Resolution: 1200x900px minimum
- Format: JPG
- Aspect ratio: 4:3
- Clean, professional photos with good lighting

These images are used in the "Our Premium Fleet" section of the homepage.
